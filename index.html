MobilTelesco: Pioneering Smartphone Astrophotography

MobilTelesco is an open-access dataset capturing the beauty of the night sky using affordable smartphone setups. This project demonstrates how modern consumer devices, paired with basic stabilization and long-exposure apps, can democratize astronomical observation and fuel citizen-led research and machine learning innovations. Accepted at CAIP 2025, our work in “Evaluating Deep Learning Models on Smartphone-Based Astrophotography” showcases the potential of this dataset.

Explore a universe captured with minimal budget, no professional telescope, and a passion for open-source science. From stars and constellations to the Milky Way, MobilTelesco invites you to contribute to accessible astronomy.

"A telescope in your pocket. The universe on your screen."
